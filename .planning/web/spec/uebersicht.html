Overview

Plan documentation for the VarLens web port. Two stages: Concept (Stage 1, minimum scope, runnable concept with test data) and Operations (Stage 2, pilot and production operation, builds on a completed concept state). Cross-cutting decisions live in the Contract, Architecture Decision Records, and Glossary.

Concept (Stage 1: Concept)

Minimum scope per the Task Profile. Runnable concept with test and fake data; real data is not permitted. The Bridge Clause guarantees that every Concept decision keeps the path to Stage 2 open.
